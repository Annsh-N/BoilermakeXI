About CampusCrunch

CampusCrunch is your culinary compass at Purdue, transforming the way students dine on campus. It's a smart meal generator that curates dining hall menus to your tastes and nutritional needs, tailoring every meal into an adventure of flavor and health. With CampusCrunch, experience the joy of effortless, varied, and delicious dining at your fingertips – because at Purdue, every meal should be as dynamic as your campus life. Boiler up, chow down!

Our Story

As a group of health-conscious students at Purdue, we found ourselves caught between the limited choices of dining hall foods – either repetitive salads or unhealthy options. Seeking a middle ground, we developed CampusCrunch. This tool is our solution for students like us, aiming to create meals that are both nutritious and satisfying, aligned with various dietary needs and health goals. It's about making every meal at Purdue a balanced and enjoyable experience.

What We Offer

Our tool is designed with a deep understanding of the varied dietary needs of students. Whether you're vegan, following a gluten-free diet, have calorie or macro goals, or are focused on high-protein foods for fitness, CampusCrunch will curate meals that suit your specific requirements. We integrate nutritional information, including calorie counts and macronutrient breakdowns, allowing you to make informed choices that align with your health and wellness goals.
